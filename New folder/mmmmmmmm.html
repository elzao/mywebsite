WINGS OF COMPASSION CHILDREN'S HOME

Wings of Compassion was founded on 1st May 2011 by Rev. Danshire and his wife, Dorcas Danshire...

Mission

To restore hope and ensure a better future for abandoned and neglected teenage mothers.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Compassion
  • Passion

Objectives

To rescue hopeless children from despair through legal channels and provide food, shelter, clothing, and medical care.

The Current Centre

A safe, dedicated rescue home providing holistic support for teenage mothers and their babies.
