Our Moments & Memories

Take a glimpse into the daily life, celebrations, and community support system at Wings of Compassion Children's Home.

Classroom Learning

Our children engaging in morning lessons, building bright foundations for their educational journeys.

Playground Smiles

Joyful afternoons spent outdoors enjoying sports, games, and team building activities with friends.

Community Support

Warm visits and food basket distributions organized by our incredible local donors and well-wishers.

Creative Arts Workshop

Nurturing self-expression and motor skills through painting, drawing, and crafting sessions.

Nutritious Mealtimes

Gathering together as a family to enjoy healthy, freshly cooked daily meals in our dining hall.

Holiday Celebrations

Making magical memories during birthday celebrations and annual festive holiday gatherings.

Sustainable Gardening

Teaching sustainable livelihood skills as children help cultivate our backyard vegetable farm.

Music & Choir Practice

The sounds of harmony fill the air during our weekly music, singing, and performance sessions.

Mentorship & Guidance

Inspirational guest mentors reading stories and teaching valuable life skills to the children.

Digital Literacy Training

Equipping our older kids with basic computer skills to open up modern educational opportunities.

Annual Sports Day

Fostering active physical development and friendly sportsmanship during our athletic field track events.

Health & Wellness Visits

Ensuring absolute well-being through professional health checkups and preventative medical camps.

Library Reading Hours

A quiet and focused space where minds expand through storybooks and curriculum materials.

Environmental Care

Instilling a love for nature by planting trees and keeping our environment beautiful and clean.

Our Big Family

A strong community bound by love, safety, and mutual support at Wings of Compassion.
