General Information

POPULATION.

As of now the home has currently EIGHTY Mothers and children.

EDUCATION.

All the girls are in school raising a concern for school fees,thoes who are not in secondary school are in short courses and acquiring skills.

RE-INTEGRATION.

All the girls are re-integrated after secondary school to the society to acquire self reliance and they are taken to college while re-integrating.
